Introduction

Welcome to Expense Tracker. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our application.

üåü Important: Expense Tracker is designed with privacy as a core principle. All your financial data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Data Collection and Storage

What We Collect

Expense Tracker collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Transaction descriptions and amounts
  • Income and expense categories
  • Transaction dates and types
  • Your financial balance calculations
üìç Local Storage Only: All your financial data is stored exclusively in your device's browser local storage. We do not have access to this information.

What We Don't Collect

  • Personal identification information
  • Bank account details
  • Location data
  • Device information
  • Usage analytics

Data Usage

The data collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Displaying your financial transactions and balances
  • Calculating income and expense summaries
  • Generating export files (Excel/PDF) for your personal use
  • Providing the core expense tracking functionality

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to any third parties. Your financial information remains private and secure on your device.

The only exception is when you choose to use the export features (Excel/PDF), which generates files stored locally on your device, or when you use the social sharing features, which follow the privacy policies of the respective platforms (Telegram, WhatsApp, Twitter).

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device and browser updated
  • Using device security features (passcodes, biometrics)
  • Being cautious when sharing exported files

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Modification: Edit or delete any transaction
  • Deletion: Clear all data by clearing your browser storage
  • Export: Download your data in Excel or PDF format

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
